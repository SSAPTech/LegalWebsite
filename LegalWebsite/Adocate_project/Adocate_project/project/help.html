Frequently Asked Questions

General Services

What legal areas do you specialize in?

+

We specialize in Family Law, Property Disputes, Criminal Defense, Civil Litigation, Corporate Law, and Legal Documentation. Our expertise covers a wide range of legal matters including divorce, child custody, property transactions, criminal cases, business law, and contract drafting.

How do I book an initial consultation?

+

You can book a consultation by calling us at +91 98765 43210, sending an email to adv.arjun@example.com, or filling out the contact form on our website. We offer both in-person and virtual consultations based on your preference and convenience.

Is the initial consultation free?

+

Yes, we offer a free initial consultation (up to 30 minutes) to understand your legal needs and assess your case. This helps us provide you with an honest evaluation and recommend the best course of action.

What should I bring to our first meeting?

+

Please bring all relevant documents related to your case, including contracts, agreements, court papers, correspondence, identification documents, and any other materials that might be relevant to your legal matter. We'll provide a specific checklist based on your case type during scheduling.

Fees & Billing

How are your legal fees structured?

+

Our fee structure varies based on the type of legal service required. We offer hourly rates for consultation and court appearances, fixed fees for document drafting and simple procedures, and contingency fees for certain types of cases. All fees are discussed transparently before any work begins.

Do you accept payment plans?

+

Yes, we understand that legal expenses can be significant. We offer flexible payment plans for qualifying cases and clients. Payment options include installments, milestone-based payments, and other arrangements that can be discussed during your consultation.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept cash, check, bank transfers, UPI payments, and major credit/debit cards. For your convenience, we also accept online payments and can set up automatic payment arrangements for ongoing cases.

Documentation

What documents are needed for a divorce case?

+

For divorce cases, you'll typically need marriage certificate, identity proofs, address proofs, income documents, property documents, photographs, evidence of grounds for divorce, and any existing agreements. We'll provide a comprehensive checklist specific to your situation.

How do I prepare for a property dispute case?

+

Gather all property documents including sale deed, registration papers, survey documents, tax receipts, possession documents, correspondence with other parties, and any agreements. Original documents or certified copies are essential for building a strong case.

Can you help with document verification?

+

Yes, we offer document verification services to ensure all your legal documents are authentic, properly executed, and legally valid. This service helps prevent future legal complications and ensures your interests are protected.

Process Timeline

How long does a typical divorce case take?

+

Divorce timelines vary significantly. Mutual consent divorces can be completed in 6-18 months, while contested divorces may take 2-5 years depending on complexity, court schedules, and cooperation between parties. We'll provide realistic timelines based on your specific situation.

What's the timeline for property registration?

+

Simple property registration can be completed in 15-30 days once all documents are ready. Complex cases involving multiple parties or disputed ownership may take longer. We handle all paperwork and coordinate with registration offices to expedite the process.

How quickly can I get bail in a criminal case?

+

Bail applications can be filed immediately upon arrest. Regular bail may be granted within 24-72 hours for bailable offenses, while anticipatory bail applications may take 1-2 weeks. We prioritize urgent criminal matters and work swiftly to secure your release.
